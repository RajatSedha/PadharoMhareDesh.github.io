Precautions

1) Testing of all staff for Covid-19 prior to start of operations and thereafter routine medical check-up of all staff at their level


2) Training of staff regarding COVID19 preventive measures


3) Details of all staff including mobile delivery staff to be maintained for back tracing


4) Thermal screening of the staff at the time of reporting


5) Regular cleaning and disinfection of the premises, of all raw material and supplies


5) Wearing of mask, head cover and hand gloves mandatory for all


6) Housekeeping staff to be provided training and material for disinfection made ensuring 1 to 1.5 meter gap between two persons


8) Hand sanitizers at all places within reach of customers


9) Que- manager to be used to manage crowd and ensure social distancing


10) Only online payment to be received to avoid man to man contact


11) Delivery of food box/ package to customers without personal contact


12) Packing material to be fully sanitised


13 Protocol for the customers shall be displayed prominently at the entrance


14) In case of overcrowding, dispersal and distancing or if needed closing the operations
